About Us

Wolf Wild World is a nature preserve dedicated to the protection of Yellowstone's wolves. Here, wolves live freely in their natural environment, maintaining the balance of the ecosystem. Visitors can observe them up close and learn about their behavior. Join our mission to conserve their habitat and preserve their future.

Adopt a Wolf

By symbolically adopting a wolf, you directly contribute to the care and maintenance of wolves in Wolf Wild World. Your donation supports food programs, protection and preservation of its natural habitat. Plus, you'll receive updates on the wolves you help protect. Join our cause and make a difference in the lives of these incredible animals!

  • You can choose between different levels of adoption, each with exclusive benefits such as photos and special reports.
  • You will receive a welcome kit with information about your adopted wolf and how you impact its life.
  • Every adoption helps fund medical care and food for the wolves at Wolf Wild World.
